The Paddock

With the Sports Only allowing 10 teams. Each team must be the top of the top to be here. Of the 10 teams that are in the sport 6 are car manafucturers, 1 is a luxury clothe brand, 1 energy drink brand and 2 independent teams. Lets explore these 10 teams and see how they top up against each other

The Energy Drink of the Champions, Its Red Bull. This Energy drink has been associated with crazy stunts and Extreme sports. So it was only a matter of time before redbull joins the paddock. This Racing team started as a title sponsor before buying out jaguar to become a formula 1 team. Now with 5 construtors championships and 6 drivers championships. It has become one of the sports greatest teams and is one of the 3 main contenders in the Sports. Founded by the late Dietrich Mateschitz. This Team has been through a lot. Under the Management of Christain Horner and Based Milton Keynes. This team has fought long and hard for its titles. This team has won its first Drivers and Constructors Championship during 2010 under brought by the legendary driver Sebastian Vettel. They went on a 4 championship streak before RedBull and Sebastian Vettel parted ways. Now 7 years later their duo of Max Versatppen and Sergio Perez Gives them another driver and constructors championship and Their story of dominance is still not over

Its the Prancing Horse of the Paddock. Scuderia Ferrari. From the luxury car brand of Ferrari. The Red team is the most prestgious Team on the track, Founded by Enzo Ferrari himself the team has won 15 Constructors Championships and 15 Drivers championships. The team has hosted the likes of Micheal Schumacher and Fernando Alonso. The team has also given many other drivers thier first Chmapionships from Kimi Raikonnen, Nikki Lauda and Alberto Ascari. The team holds the many records for its brilliance. Most Constructors, Most Drivers and so much more. It is also supplies other teams with its engines. These teams being Alphatauri, haas and Alfa Romeo. Its the second team of the three main contenders in this sport. With its roster of Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz there is no way they will back down from a fight

Its the silver arrows. Mercedes-AMG. Brought from braun mercedes is the latest team to join the grid and its the third of the three main contenders in the sports. From being a Engine producer to a Full time F1 team. Mercedes has been through a lot. Being the team with the longest Consecutive Constructors champions and Tied with The Longest Consecutive Drivers Championship it is a team that you wouldnt turn a blind eye to. With the likes of Lewis Hamilton and George Russel and being driven by other big names like Nico Rosberg and Micheal Schumacher. It is one of the most succesful teams in mercedes. It also supplies engines to Mclaren, Aston-Martin and Williams. With a great roster and a little Silver dust it will always be a close battle

With 10 Constructors its the orange lines of Mclaren. From its the Luxury car brand. This team is one of the oldest teams in the sports. Sporting the Honey Badger of Daniel Riccardo and Lando Norris. There will never be a dull moment in the team. Its Mercedes powered cars bring it up to the Mid-field and with its History the team will always push for perfection. Having hosted some big names like Lewis Hamilton, Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost and Emerson Fittipaldi. It will always be an Honor to drive for the Orange Team

Its the French Team of Alpine. Named renault before being rebranded. Alpine is another one of these big names in the sports. With the Duo of Fernando Alonso and Esteban Ocon Expect a tough fight in the midfield. Before being Alpine the team was under the name of Renault it won 2 championships.
